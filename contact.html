What We Can Help With

Our team is happy to assist with the following types of inquiries:

  • Content corrections — If you notice any inaccurate, outdated, or misleading information on our site, please let us know with the URL and details of the issue. We take accuracy seriously and will review and correct any legitimate errors promptly.
  • Topic suggestions — Have a question about insurance that you think deserves a detailed article? We welcome suggestions for new content that would benefit our readers.
  • General feedback — We value hearing about your experience using our site. Your feedback helps us improve our guides and tools.
  • Advertising inquiries — For questions about advertising on InsuranceCostGuide, please include "Advertising" in the subject line of your email.
  • Privacy and data requests — For requests related to your personal data under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy regulations, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us with "Privacy Request" in the subject line.

What We Cannot Help With

InsuranceCostGuide is an educational resource. We are not licensed insurance agents, brokers, or advisors, and we cannot provide the following:

  • Personalized insurance recommendations or quotes
  • Assistance with filing insurance claims
  • Advice on specific policy terms or coverage disputes
  • Referrals to specific insurance companies or agents

If you need personalized assistance with insurance, we recommend contacting a licensed insurance agent or broker in your state. You can find licensed agents through your state's Department of Insurance website or through the National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC).

Response Time

We aim to respond to all emails within two business days. During high-volume periods, such as open enrollment season for health insurance, responses may take slightly longer. Rest assured that we read every email we receive and will get back to you as soon as possible.
